Lung Cancer Risk Check

A few quick questions about screening eligibility

Question 1

What's your current age?

Lung cancer screening guidelines focus on adults aged 50 to 80.

Question 2

Have you ever smoked cigarettes regularly?

This helps determine whether lung cancer screening guidelines apply to you.

Question 3

About how much have you smoked?

We'll estimate your "pack-years." One pack-year = 1 pack a day for 1 year (or 2 packs/day for 6 months, etc.).

Enter both to estimate pack-years.
Question 4

How many years ago did you quit?

Screening guidelines focus on those who quit within the past 15 years.
