Sources & References

The evidence behind CancerLess's breast cancer risk information

CancerLess's educational content and risk estimates are grounded in guidelines and resources from the leading authorities on breast cancer below. This app is for education only and is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your physician about your personal health.
About the risk estimate. The breast cancer risk check uses a hybrid screening model built on validated, peer-reviewed tools — primarily the NCI Breast Cancer Risk Assessment Tool (BCRAT) and its published race- and ethnicity-specific recalibrations. No single model is perfect for every individual, and this estimate does not include genetic (BRCA), breast-density, or extended family-history factors. If your result is elevated or you have a strong family history, please talk with your doctor about a full clinical assessment.
